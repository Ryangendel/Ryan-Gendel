About Me

As recient transplant to the great city of Chicago I have been taking advantange of everything Chicago has to offer. The food is amazing, the higher education institutions cannot be beat, and the people here are awesome.

Before I moved out to this city I worked at M.S. University of Denver's media office and was in charge of the creative department. Through this I learned how to edit video, write stories for television news, create online and print ads, and layout newspapers. Although I majored in economics I have found myself working on creative endevours professionally.
